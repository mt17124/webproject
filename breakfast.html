Breakfast quinoa bowl with fried egg

Ingredients:

1. 4 slices bacon, chopped

2. 1/2 onion, sliced

3. 1 cup quinoa, cooked

4. 1 handful arugula

5. 1 tablespoon olive oil

6. 2 eggs

Instructions:

1. Add bacon to a pan on medium-high heat and cook until golden brown and fat has rendered (4-6 minutes). Remove and set aside.

2. Caramelize the onion in the same pan on medium-high heat for 4-6 minutes. Add the bacon back in. Fold in the quinoa to heat through for 1 minute.

3. Portion the quinoa mix into bowls. Top with the arugula.

4. Add oil to a small frying pan. Fry the two eggs then add to the top of the quinoa bowls. Crack open the yolks and enjoy!

Simple overnight oats

Ingredients:

1. 1/cup oats

2. 1/2 cup semi-skimmed milk (you can add any milk of your liking)

3. 1/4 cup fruit yoghurt

4. 1/2 cup fruit (strawberry, raspberry, bluberry, kiwi, mango)

4. 1-3 tsp honey or maple syrup (optional)

5. Chia seeds (optional)

6. Peanut butter (optiona)

7. Coconut flakes (optional)

Instructions:

1. Add oats, milk, yogurt, fruit(s) of your choice to a 16 oz. jar or bowl. Add optional mix-ins listed for desired flavor (don't add toppings until the next day). Stir then cover and refrigerate overnight.

Fluffly morning pancakes

Ingredients:

1. 2 1/2 tsps baking powder

2. 2 large eggs

3. 225g plain flour

4. 1 pinch of salt

5. 300ml of milk

6. 30 grams butter

7. Bananas, strawberries (optional)

Instructions:

1. The easiest way to make these is to put all the ingredients into a blender and blitz. But if you do mix up the batter by hand in a bowl, make a well in the flour, baking powder, salt, beat in the eggs, melted butter and milk, and transfer to a jug: it's much easier to pour the batter into the pan than to spoon it.

2. Heat a smooth griddle or pan on the stove.

3. When you cook the pancakes, all you need to remember is that when the upper side of the pancake is blistering and bubbling it's time to cook the second side, and this needs only about 1 minute, if that. This recipe will produce 16 silver-dollar-sized pancakes.

4. Once ready, present on a plate and pour maple or chocolate syrup over the pancakes. Add optional toppings for desired taste. It is ready to be devoured!
